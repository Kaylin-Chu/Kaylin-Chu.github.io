Te Whare Tapa Wha

I usually refer to the model as 'Whare Tapa Wha', the model is based on a house not being able to stand without all 4 walls and often includes the land the house stands on. Technically a house could stand if one wall had been destoryed but it would eventually decay further if not fixed. Whare tapa wha focuses on working all 4 walls/wellbeings to be the strongest house/person one can be.
