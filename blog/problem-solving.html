Blocked on a simple problem

A really simple problem I got stuck on was during the flipflop task. The flipflop task aims to write a function where multiples of 3 are replaced with 'flip', multiples of 5 are replaced with 'flop' and multiples of 15 are replaced with 'flipflop'. I have attached a picture of the intended output

I put the 15 division parameter last and I couldn't understand why the file wasn't returning flipflop and just kept returning flip. Eventually, I figured out I had ordered the conditions incorrectly. I used the error messages to solve the issue, the error message said the expected result 'flipflop' did not match the returned result 'flip'. I also sort of used asking your peers for help by checking the Discord threads to double check my code. It was nice to solve the issue and completely understand the solution. I learned the 15 had to go first because 3 and 5 go into 15 but 15 doesn't go into 3 and 5. The code is read top to bottom so makes sense that 15 must go first.
